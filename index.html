About Sportat

Sportat is a powerful sports platform designed to make your fitness journey easier and more enjoyable. Whether you’re booking facilities, finding top trainers, or engaging with a vibrant sports community, Sportat has everything in one place. With each booking, you earn Energy Points that can be redeemed for discounts or future services. Stay motivated by connecting with others through posts and short video reels, and shop for high-quality sports gear directly from the app. Sportat brings everything you need for an active lifestyle together in a simple, rewarding way. Join us and elevate your sports experience!

Book Fields and Manage Subscriptions

Sportat simplifies the process of booking sports fields and managing your memberships, offering a seamless experience for all users. Whether you're looking to reserve a football pitch, basketball court, tennis court, or even a swimming pool, Sportat provides access to a variety of facilities at the best locations. With real-time availability and instant confirmation, you can easily book your preferred venue for training, practice, or friendly matches.

In addition to field bookings, Sportat also offers a range of subscriptions to suit your fitness needs. Whether you're interested in monthly gym memberships, swimming class packages, or yearly access to sports clubs, you can explore and choose the plan that best fits your schedule and goals.

For example, you can subscribe to a 6-month swimming program at your local club, a monthly football field membership that offers discounted bookings for weekly games, or an annual fitness package that provides unlimited access to the gym and group classes.

With a variety of flexible subscription options, Sportat ensures that you have full control over how you engage with sports and fitness, making it easier than ever to stay active. Whether it's booking a court or signing up for a membership, Sportat provides a streamlined, stress-free experience to help you focus on what matters most—playing and staying fit!

Sportar Energy Points

Energy Points is Sportat’s unique rewards system that motivates you to stay active and engaged. Every time you book a facility, attend a class, or participate in app activities, you accumulate points. These points can be redeemed for discounts on future bookings, exclusive deals, or purchases from the in-app sports store. The more you use Sportat, the more points you earn, allowing you to unlock exciting rewards and offers. With Energy Points, Sportat makes your fitness journey not only enjoyable but also rewarding. Stay active, collect points, and reap the benefits as you achieve your fitness goals!
